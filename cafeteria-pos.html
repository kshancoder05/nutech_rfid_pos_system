Link a card to a student

Scanned card UID— tap a card —

Tap the student's new card on the reader, type their Student ID, then link. This writes the card's UID onto that student's record so future taps identify them automatically. Re-enrolling replaces a lost card.

Fingerprint (optional second factor)

After the card is linked, capture the student's fingerprint on the DigitalPersona 4500 to require it at checkout. Biometric data — collect written consent first.
